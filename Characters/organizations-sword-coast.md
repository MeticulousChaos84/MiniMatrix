# Organizations of the Sword Coast

 

**Sources:** Sword Coast Adventurer's Guide, Campaign Setting Box Set Revised, Forgotten Realms Campaign Guide

## Overview

 

The Sword Coast and surrounding regions are home to numerous organizations that operate across national boundaries, pursuing their own agendas and shaping the fate of Faerûn. These range from benevolent secret societies to ruthless criminal networks, from political alliances to religious orders.

 

---

 

## The Lords' Alliance

 

### Purpose and Philosophy

 

The Lords' Alliance is a confederation of rulers of towns and cities across the North, who have pledged peace with one another and promised to share information and effort against common threats such as orc hordes and Northlander pirates.

 

> "It is a loose confederation of those settlements and their agents, all of whom owe allegiance first to their homelands, and second to the Lords' Alliance." - Sword Coast Adventurer's Guide

 

### Founding Principle

 

The Alliance operates under the principle that communities with common cause that engage in trade are less likely to go to war with one another.

### Primary Opposition

 

The Alliance exists primarily as a counterforce to the Zhentarim and their expansionist ambitions.

## The Harpers (Those Who Harp)

 

### Purpose

 

The Harpers are a secret organization dedicated to preserving balance, fighting tyranny, and protecting the Realms.

 

> "The Harpers are a force of good in the Realms, and good-aligned characters may find themselves being aided by unseen allies if their business aids the organization." - Campaign Setting Box Set Revised

 

### Membership

 

- Bards (predominant)

- Druids

- Mages

- Priests

- Rangers

 

Membership is by invitation only.

 

### Notable Members

 

- Khelben Arunsun

- Elminster

- Mourngrym of Shadowdale

- Lord Piergeiron of Waterdeep

 

### Centers of Operation

 

- **Berdusk** - Twilight Hall

- **Shadowdale** - Powerful allies

- **Silverymoon** - Safe haven

 

### Symbol

 

The harp and moon.

## The Zhentarim (The Black Network)

 

### Overview

 

An organization of evil priests, wizards, and creatures bent on controlling trade and power between the Sword Coast and the Moonsea.

 

### Relationship with Zhentil Keep

 

> "Much confusion exists in the Realms regarding Zhentil Keep and the (not-so) secret society known as the Black Network or Zhentarim. The two are closely tied." - Campaign Setting Box Set Revised

 

### Power Centers

 

- Zhentil Keep (original base)

- Citadel of the Raven

- Darkhold

 

### Methods

 

- Agent networks

- Trade monopolies

- Military force

- Alliances with evil creatures

 

### Historical Actions

 

- Destroyed Teshendale

- Nearly destroyed Daggerdale

- Placed agents throughout the Dales

## Other Notable Organizations

 

### The Seven Sisters

 

Seven sisters blessed by Mystra with great power and long life:

- Storm Silverhand

- Dove Falconhand

- The Simbul

- Alustriel

- Laeral

- Sylune (deceased)

- Unknown seventh

 

### Cult of the Dragon

 

Venerates dragons, particularly dracoliches. Active in the Cold Lands and North.

 

### Red Wizards of Thay

 

Seek to establish Thay as superior magical power in Faerûn.
