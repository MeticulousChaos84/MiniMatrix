# Volo's Guard at the Cage

**Source:** `GOB_VoloBallad_Guard_AtCage.html`
**Starting Node:** Variable

---

An interaction with the goblin guard stationed at Volo's cage in the Goblin Camp. The famous bard Volothamp Geddarm has been captured and imprisoned by the goblins, and this guard is watching over him.

**Key Elements:**
- A goblin guarding Volo's cage
- The guard can be persuaded, deceived, or distracted
- Various ways to get past or deal with the guard
- Volo needs rescuing from the goblins

**Player Approaches:**
- Persuade the guard to leave
- Deceive them about your intentions
- Intimidate them away
- Distract them with something
- Simply attack

This dialogue is part of the Volo rescue questline, where players must find a way to free the famous (and famously reckless) bard from goblin captivity.
