# Counsellor Florrick at Waukeen's Rest

**Source:** `PLA_Desire.html`
**Starting Node:** 167

---

After rescuing Counsellor Florrick from the burning Waukeen's Rest inn, she reveals crucial information about the kidnapping of Grand Duke Ulder Ravengard of Baldur's Gate by drow raiders.

**Synopsis:**
Florrick works in secret for a dragon in Baldur's Gate and serves as an advisor to Grand Duke Ravengard. When rescued from the fire, she immediately takes charge, ordering the surviving Flaming Fists to seek reinforcements. She reveals that drow attacked in the night and took the Grand Duke westward.

**Key Moments:**
- Florrick: "A scorched throat, a few hairs singed off. Nothing a bit of time and fresh air can't cure."
- Florrick: "Gauntlet, a new duty calls. Drow have taken Grand Duke Ulder Ravengard - westward, if my eyes and ears can be believed."
- Florrick: "Report to the manip and send for reinforcements. We must find the Duke."

**Wyll's Connection:**
If Wyll is present, this scene becomes deeply personal:
- Wyll: "Counsellor Florrick - are you all right?"
- Florrick: "Yes, Wyll. The drow have your father."
- Wyll reveals that Ravengard is his father, though their relationship is complicated
- Wyll: "The circumstance of my birth is no matter of pride for neither me nor my father."

**Quest Hooks:**
- Rescue Duke Ravengard from his captors
- Learn that he was taken westward (toward Moonrise Towers)
- Florrick offers a reward at Wyrm's Rock if the player helps

**Player Choices:**
- Agree to search for Duke Ravengard (Gale +1, Wyll +10, Karlach +5)
- Refuse to help (Astarion +1, Wyll -5, Karlach -1)
- Ask about rewards
