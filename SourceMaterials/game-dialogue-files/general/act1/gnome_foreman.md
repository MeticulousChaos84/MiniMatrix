# Gnome Foreman - Slave Overseer Dialogue

**Source:** `UND_GnomeForeman.html`
**Starting Node:** Various

---

This dialogue involves the gnome foreman at the Grymforge excavation site. The gnomes are enslaved by the duergar and forced to dig to free True Soul Nere from the cave-in.

The foreman is a deep gnome slave who has been forced into a position of authority over his fellow gnomes by the duergar overseers. He exists in an impossible situation - responsible for motivating his people to work while knowing that their labor serves their captors.

The player can:
- Learn about the excavation progress
- Discover the situation with the trapped Nere
- Gather information about slave conditions
- Learn about escape attempts or resistance

The foreman provides context for the desperate situation the gnome slaves face, caught between the duergar's cruelty and Nere's demands for freedom.
