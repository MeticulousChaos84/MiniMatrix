# The Unfortunate Gnome

**Source:** `UND_UnfortunateGnome.html`
**Starting Node:** Various

---

This dialogue involves one of the enslaved deep gnomes at the Grymforge - likely Beldron, one of the gnomes working to free Nere from the cave-in. The "unfortunate" title references their grim situation under duergar captivity.

The gnome is exhausted, terrified, and desperate. They've been worked nearly to death digging through rubble while poisonous gas seeps through the cracks. Time is running out - if they don't free Nere soon, he'll die, and the duergar will take out their frustration on the slaves.

Key dialogue topics:
- The desperate situation with the cave-in
- Fear of both Nere and the duergar overseers
- The fate of escaped gnomes (Philomeen, Thulla)
- What happens to slaves who fail

The player can:
- Learn about the excavation's purpose
- Discover ways to help or sabotage
- Offer hope or false comfort
- Learn about other gnome prisoners

This dialogue provides crucial context for the moral choices ahead - freeing Nere will let him continue his tyranny, but letting him die means the gnomes suffer the consequences.
