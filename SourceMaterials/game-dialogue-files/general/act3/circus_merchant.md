# Circus of the Last Days - Popper the Merchant

**Source:** `WYR_Circus_Merchant.html`
**Starting Node:** 22

---

Popper is a kobold merchant at the Circus of the Last Days who sells magical items, snacks, and "junks" he finds on dead people. After the Dribbles fight, he also has Dribbles' severed hand for sale.

**Popper's Introduction:**
"Oh, oh! Hungry little hobgoblin. Want a treato?"

"I has lots of treatos! And magics. And junks I finds. Don't worry - I only takes junks from dead people. I is nice."

**Race-Specific Greetings:**
- To Dragonborn: "Oh, oh! A scaly one! Hungry lizard. *Big* lizard. Want a treato?"
- To rare races: "Oh, oh! A strange one. A never-seen-before one. Hungry? Want a treato?"

**About Looting Corpses:**
"Lots of times! The beasts ate a kiddo the other day and I got some good stuffs. Uh, I mean, 'Circus is safe, do not a-worry!' Yes."

**Cleric/Paladin Response:**
Player: "Graverobbing is a profane act. Makes you *stink*, too."
Popper: "Shut up, stupid sweat-piss-incense smeller!"
(Approval: Astarion -1, all others +1)

## Dribbles' Hand Quest

After the doppelganger fight, Popper possesses Dribbles' severed hand - a quest item needed for the Ringmaster.

**About the Hand:**
"I founds it by the clown man's tent. Fought a rat for it and everythings. Hueh."

"It's a one-of-a-kind hand with *artisanal* bite marks. It's worth LOTS."

**Getting the Hand:**

**Buy It:**
"I, uh, don't know nuthin' about no Dribbles. You wants it - you gots to pays for it!"
(Very expensive - he's a "tough businesslord")

**Persuasion (DC 15):** "I think that belongs to Dribbles. I need it - your ringmaster needs it."
Success: "All *right*. Mama Lucretious says we family - s'pose that means Dribbles was, too. 'Ere. But don't tell nobodies I gaves it to you for free - gotta protect my reputation as a tough businesslord."
(Approval: All companions +1)

**Deception (DC 15):** "You know, I can get a hand for *much* cheaper elsewhere."
Success: "Urgh. Fine. I gives it to you - for a discount! HAHA."
(Approval: All companions +1)

**Deception (DC 15) - Wizard/Warlock/Sorcerer:** "I'm actually a sorcerer working for Lucretious. I need that hand for a magic spell."
Success: "Aww magic peoples gets the best stuff. *Fine.*"

**Race-Specific Intimidation:**

**Drow (DC 10):** "You will give me that hand or I will grind you beneath my heel, you insufferable gnat."
Success: "O-of course. Anything for a most beautiful and fancy drow person. Here. Popper hopes you like it..."
Failure: "N-no! Popper is a tough businesslord! No free stuffs, even for a - a beautiful drow person!"
(Popper has a crush on Drow characters)
(Approval: All companions +1)

**Dragonborn (DC 10):** "I will rip out your claws and peel the scales from your flesh. Give it to me - *now.*"
Success: "O-of course. Anything for a scaley scale. Here. Popper hopes you like it..."
(Popper admires Dragonborn)
(Approval: All companions +1)

**Half-Orc (DC 15):** "I could crush you with one finger. Give me the hand - now."
Failure: "Hoho! SPICY. But Popper only likes getting smooshed by spicy drow, not orcy orcs. Sorry."
(Approval: All companions +1)

**Standard Intimidation (DC 15):** "Give it to me now, or I'll cut off *your* hands."
Success: "Ew. I don'ts like kinky shit. Here. Take the hand. Weirdo."
Failure: "Cut Popper's hands into juicy bits if you likes - Mama Lucretious can make new fingies for me!"
(Approval: All companions +1)

**Violence Option:**
Player: "I'd rather take it from your cold, dead corpse."
Popper: "HAH. COME ON. I'LL EATS YOUR FACE." (Combat)

**If Player Steals the Hand:**
Popper: "OI! GIVE THAT BACK! It's my special hand!"

To Dragonborn: "OI! GIVE THAT - oh, a scaly scale! That's - that's my super special hand and I needs it back. Uh, now!"

To Drow: "OI! GIVE THAT - oh, a fancy drow person! That's - that's my super special hand and I needs it back. Uh, now!"

If player refuses to return it: "POPPER HATES SNEAKY LITTLE THIEFS - EVEN BEAUTIFUL DROW THIEFS!" (Combat, but he's conflicted about it)

**Post-Combat Greeting:**
"Clown man is dead. Oh well. Want a treato?"

Player: "That's a little callous."
Popper: "Why? I die. You die. We all die. Big woof! Have a treato - it'll make it better."
(Approval: Astarion -1, Lae'zel -1, Minthara -1 / Gale +1, Shadowheart +1, Wyll +1, Karlach +1, Jaheira +1, Minsc +1, Halsin +1)
