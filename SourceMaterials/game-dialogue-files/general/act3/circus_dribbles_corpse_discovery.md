# Finding Dribbles the Clown's Corpse

**Source:** `WYR_Circus_PAD_FoundClownCorpse.html`
**Starting Node:** 0

---

An automated dialogue that triggers each time the party picks up a piece of Dribbles the Clown's dismembered body. The companions comment on what they find and track how many pieces remain to be collected.

The player can find Dribbles' body parts scattered around various locations after learning from Lucretious that the clown has gone missing. Each discovery prompts a reaction from the party about the gruesome find.
