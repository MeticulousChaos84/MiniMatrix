# Last Light Inn - Isobel (Default Dialogue)

**Source:** `HAV_Isobel.html`
**Starting Node:** Various (state-dependent)

---

## Overview

This is Isobel's default dialogue at Last Light Inn, available after initial meetings (BriefAtLake, BriefInRoom, and SpyCapture). The dialogue varies greatly depending on the state of the inn and what the player has discovered in Moonrise Towers or the Shar Temple. Isobel is the Selunite cleric whose blessing protects Last Light from the Shadow Curse.

## Trigger

Available after initial plot dialogues with Isobel have been resolved.

## Key Topics

Isobel can discuss:
- The nature of her protective barrier
- Her history with Ketheric Thorm (her father)
- The Shadow Curse and how it fell
- Dame Aylin (the Nightsong)
- The player's mission to Moonrise Towers

## State-Dependent Variations

The dialogue changes based on:
- Whether the player has visited Moonrise Towers
- Whether they've discovered secrets in the Shar Temple
- Whether Marcus has been revealed as a traitor
- Whether Dame Aylin has been freed
- The general mood state of Last Light Inn (Protected, Alert, etc.)
