## Key Debates in the Fandom

### Things People AGREE On:
- Gale has an inferiority-superiority complex
- His arrogance masks insecurity
- The Mystra relationship has problematic power dynamics
- His verbosity is both genuine expression and defense mechanism
- His good ending is the professor ending

### Things People DEBATE:
- Whether his romance is "cringe" or intentionally awkward
- How much he's a victim vs. responsible for his choices
- Whether his demands (magic items) make him annoying or interesting
- The extent to which he's aware of his own manipulation
- Whether Mystra was abusive or just complicated

### Underexplored Areas:
- His relationship with his mother and childhood
- How his magic-sensing works as a character trait
- His poetry and what it reveals about him
- His friendships with non-romantic companions
- How his academic background shapes his worldview
