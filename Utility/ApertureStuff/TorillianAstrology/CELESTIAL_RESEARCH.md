## The Big Picture: How Torillian Astrology Differs from Earth

**KEY INSIGHT**: Faer√ªn doesn't have a traditional zodiac like Earth's 12 signs.

Instead, individuals are marked by:
- **Guiding stars** - A specific star that influences your life path
- **Falling stars** - Comets or meteor showers at birth
- **Conjunctions of constellations** - Especially rare or "fell-reputation" ones

This is MORE NARRATIVE and MORE PERSONAL than "you're a Leo."

Your Torillian birth chart would say something like: "Born under the conjunction of Mystra's Star Circle and the Far Traveler, when Anadia was bright in the west and the Tears of Sel√ªne were visible."

## The Celestial Bodies of Realmspace

### The Sun
Toril's sun is simply called "the sun" - no special name in most sources. The Bedine call it At'ar.

### Sel√ªne (The Moon)

Toril's only natural moon. Approximately 2,000 miles across, 183,000 miles from Toril.

**Astrological Domains:**
- Navigation
- Prophecy
- Cycles
- Emotions
- Questers
- Lycanthropes

**Special Features:**
- Only one side ever faces Toril (tidal locked)
- The visible side is actually an ILLUSION - beneath it is a lush, inhabited world called Leira
- The far side (true "dark side") can never be seen from Toril

**For the Framework:** Sel√ªne phases would work like Earth moon signs - emotional nature, intuition, inner self.

### The Tears of Sel√ªne

An asteroid cluster that follows Sel√ªne in orbit. Hundreds of asteroids circling each other around a focal point called "the Castle."

**Origin Story:** Dragons created a magic weapon to destroy the King-Killer Star (a dangerous comet), but they MISSED and hit Sel√ªne instead!

**For the Framework:** The Tears could represent:
- Trauma that shapes us
- Scattered pieces that need integration
- Following/devotion patterns
- Secondary emotional influences

### The Visible Planets (Five Wanderers)

Only five planets are visible to the naked eye from Toril:
- Sel√ªne (moon)
- Anadia
- Coliar
- Karpri
- Chandos

**Anadia and Coliar** are sometimes called the "Dawn Heralds" - they appear close to the sun and are never seen far from it in the sky (like Mercury and Venus from Earth).

## The Constellations

### Complete List of Known Faer√ªnian Constellations

1. **Mystra's Star Circle** / Crown of the North / Cold Crown
   - Location: Due north
   - Feature: Circle of bright stars with UTTER DARKNESS in center
   - The Brow Star marks true north
   - Missing star: N'landroshien ("Darkness in Light") - featured in elven apocalypse myths
   - Bedine: N'asr's Tent (Lord of the Dead) or Circle of Swords

2. **The Arrows of the Gods** / Caltrop / Adarivael / At'ar's Arrows
   - Three converging lines with star clusters at points
   - Marks true EAST
   - Elves hold it sacred to Solonor Thelandira (god of archery)

3. **Eyes of the Watching Woman** / Double Daggers / Two Jambiyas / Eyes of Elah
   - Marks true WEST
   - Vigilance and observation

4. **The Swordsman** / Warrior / Sentinel
   - Universal meaning across cultures
   - Loyalty, guardianship, battle, freedom
   - Associated with Torm or Tempus

5. **Amaunator's Belt**
   - Sun god's belt
   - Order, time, law

6. **Auroth the Ice Snake**
   - Northern constellation
   - Cold, winter, the north

7. **Centaur**
   - Unknown specific meaning

8. **Correlian**
   - Unknown specific meaning

9. **Elfmaid's Tears**
   - Likely associated with elven sorrow or beauty
   - Could relate to lost Myth Drannor?

10. **Far Traveler**
    - Journeys, quests, wandering
    - Perfect for adventurers

11. **Huntsman**
    - Pursuit, tracking, hunting
    - Likely associated with Malar or good hunting deities

12. **Jassa's Dagger**
    - Unknown specific meaning

13. **Lavarandar's Lantern**
    - Light in darkness
    - Guidance, hope

14. **Sleepless Knights**
    - Vigilance, protection
    - Those who watch through the night

15. **Woman Warrior**
    - Female strength and power
    - Possibly associated with Red Knight or Tempus

### Constellations by Direction

**North:** Mystra's Star Circle, Auroth the Ice Snake
**East:** Arrows of the Gods
**West:** Eyes of the Watching Woman

## Framework Design Ideas

### Core Components

1. **Guiding Star** - Based on birth date, which constellation was prominent
2. **Sun Position** - Which constellation the sun was "in" (like sun signs)
3. **Sel√ªne Phase** - Moon phase at birth (emotional nature)
4. **Tears Position** - Where the asteroid cluster was (trauma/devotion patterns)
5. **Planetary Positions** - Which constellations the 7 planets were in

### Special Modifiers

- **Far Realm stars** in aspect = shadow influences
- **Comets visible at birth** = special destiny markers
- **Conjunctions** = when multiple planets/stars align

### Interpretation Layers

1. **Basic Reading** - Sun constellation + Sel√ªne phase
2. **Full Chart** - All planetary positions
3. **Synastry** - How two charts interact
4. **Progressed** - How chart changes over time
